One of our most popular attractions is our Eagle Eye View hot air balloon. From this vantage point look down on all Sunny Acres’ many attractions! Eagle Eye has been giving aerial tours to people for 20 years and is still going strong! Make sure to stop by this amazing attraction the first chance you get!

The Ye Olde Pye Shop is a medieval themed pie shop that sells pies of all shapes and sizes! However, while we sell many kinds of pie, our specialty is rabbit meat pies! At this one stop pie shop you can have an egg and sausage pie for breakfast, a cheesy macaroni pie for lunch, and a potato and steak pie for dinner. Wrap it all up with a delicious chocolate pie for dessert. If you’re hungry we’ve got a pie for you.

Berry Pie (Blueberry, Blackberry, Raspberry): $15

Wild Berry: $20

Peach: $20

Meat Pie: $30

What kind of rabbit farm would we be without a good petting zoo? Not much of one is the answer! Our petting farm is easily one of our most popular attractions and it’s easy to understand why! Not only do we have goats, donkeys, chickens, and alpacas but we also have rabbits of all shapes and sizes! From Angora rabbits with their fluffy fur to Highland Hares with their strong bodies we’ve got them all!

$2 per child.

10$ per adult.

$15 per senior (ages 50+)

One thing that differentiates us from every other petting zoo on the planet is our unique view on food! At other petting zoos they let you feed the animals. At our petting zoo the animals can feed you! For the right price any animal you can catch will be your next meal! Just give our world class chefs some time with your catch and we’ll present it to you as a delicious entrée.

Prices start at $350.
